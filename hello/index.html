The Many Faces of "Hello"

In the real world, "hello" comes in countless forms. It's a smile across a room, a wave from a friend, a handshake between colleagues, or a simple nod on the street. These small gestures connect us in profound ways.

The Warm Embrace

A hug transcends language, conveying hello, welcome, and affection all at once. It's a physical hello that connects us deeply.

The Firm Handshake

A handshake is a formal hello that signifies respect and agreement. It's a universal gesture in professional settings.

The Friendly Wave

A wave is a versatile hello—casual yet warm. It can greet a neighbor, say goodbye, or acknowledge someone across a room.
